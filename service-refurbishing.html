Refurbishing Services

Restore Your Screen Enclosure to Like-New Condition

Complete Screen Enclosure Refurbishing

Give your existing screen enclosure a new lease on life with our comprehensive refurbishing services. We can restore aging structures to like-new condition, addressing frame repairs, screen replacement, hardware updates, and structural improvements.

Our refurbishing process includes a thorough inspection, necessary repairs, and complete restoration. We can transform worn, damaged enclosures into beautiful, functional spaces that you'll enjoy for years to come.

What We Refurbish

Frame Repairs: Fix damaged, rusted, or weakened frame components to restore structural integrity.

Screen Replacement: Replace all worn or damaged screens with new, high-quality materials.

Hardware Updates: Replace worn hardware, hinges, and fasteners with new, durable components.

Structural Improvements: Reinforce weak areas and make improvements to enhance durability and functionality.

Complete Restoration: Full refurbishment that brings your entire enclosure back to like-new condition.

Our Refurbishing Process

1. Comprehensive Inspection: We thoroughly inspect your enclosure to identify all areas that need attention.

2. Detailed Assessment: We provide a detailed assessment and quote for all necessary work.

3. Professional Repairs: Our skilled team performs all repairs and replacements with precision and care.

4. Quality Finishing: We complete the refurbishment with attention to detail, ensuring a beautiful, professional finish.

Benefits of Refurbishing

Cost-Effective: Refurbishing is often more cost-effective than complete replacement while delivering excellent results.

Extended Lifespan: Proper refurbishing can extend the life of your enclosure by many years.

Improved Functionality: Restore full functionality and improve the performance of your enclosure.

Enhanced Appearance: Transform the look of your enclosure, making it look new again.
