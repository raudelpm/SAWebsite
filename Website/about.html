Our Story

SCREEN ARMORS was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners with the highest quality screen enclosure services while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship, integrity, and customer service. We specialize in creating beautiful, durable screen enclosures that enhance outdoor living spaces and protect families from insects and the elements.

With years of combined experience in the screen enclosure industry, our team has built a reputation for excellence. We've worked on hundreds of projects, from small screen replacements to large-scale pool cage constructions, always delivering results that exceed our customers' expectations.

What Sets Us Apart

Expert Craftsmanship: Our team consists of skilled professionals who take pride in every detail of their work. We use only premium materials and proven techniques to ensure your screen enclosure stands the test of time.

Comprehensive Services: From new construction to refurbishing existing structures, we offer a complete range of services to meet all your screen enclosure needs. Whether you need a pool cage, lanai, or custom enclosure, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

Customer-Focused Approach: We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers. That's why we take the time to understand your needs, provide honest recommendations, and ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

Licensed & Insured: We operate as a fully licensed and insured business, giving you peace of mind that your project is in professional hands. We maintain all necessary certifications and follow all local building codes and regulations.

Our Commitment

At SCREEN ARMORS, we're committed to excellence in everything we do. We understand that your home is one of your most valuable investments, and we treat every project with the care and attention to detail it deserves. Our goal is not just to complete a job, but to create a screen enclosure that you'll enjoy for years to come.

We stand behind our work with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. If you're not completely happy with our work, we'll make it right. That's the SCREEN ARMORS guarantee.
