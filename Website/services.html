Our Services

Comprehensive Screen Enclosure Solutions

At SCREEN ARMORS, we offer a complete range of services to meet all your screen enclosure needs. From new construction to refurbishing existing structures, our expert team delivers quality craftsmanship and exceptional service.

Construction

We specialize in building custom screen enclosures from the ground up. Our construction services include pool cages, lanais, and patio enclosures designed to your exact specifications. We work with you to create a structure that complements your home's architecture while providing maximum protection and enjoyment of your outdoor space.

Our construction process includes site preparation, frame installation, and professional finishing. We use only premium materials and follow all local building codes to ensure your new enclosure is safe, durable, and beautiful.

Screening

Professional screen installation and replacement services for existing structures. Whether you need to replace damaged screens, upgrade to better materials, or install screens on a new frame, our team has the expertise to get the job done right.

We offer various screen types including standard fiberglass, pet-resistant screens, and solar screens. Our screening services ensure a tight, secure fit that keeps insects out while maintaining excellent airflow and visibility.

Refurbishing

Give your existing screen enclosure a new lease on life with our comprehensive refurbishing services. We can restore aging structures to like-new condition, addressing frame repairs, screen replacement, hardware updates, and structural improvements.

Our refurbishing process includes a thorough inspection, necessary repairs, and complete restoration. We can transform worn, damaged enclosures into beautiful, functional spaces that you'll enjoy for years to come.
