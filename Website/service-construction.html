Custom Screen Enclosures Built to Last

Professional Screen Enclosure Construction

At SCREEN ARMORS, we specialize in building custom screen enclosures from the ground up. Our construction services include pool cages, lanais, and patio enclosures designed to your exact specifications. We work with you to create a structure that complements your home's architecture while providing maximum protection and enjoyment of your outdoor space.

Our construction process includes site preparation, frame installation, and professional finishing. We use only premium materials and follow all local building codes to ensure your new enclosure is safe, durable, and beautiful.

What We Build

Pool Cages: Complete pool enclosure systems that protect your pool area from insects, debris, and weather while maintaining beautiful views and natural light.

Lanais: Covered outdoor living spaces that extend your home's living area, perfect for entertaining and relaxation.

Patio Enclosures: Custom-designed enclosures that transform your patio into a year-round usable space.

Custom Structures: Unique screen enclosures tailored to your specific needs and property layout.

Our Construction Process

1. Consultation & Design: We start with a free consultation to understand your needs and vision. Our team will provide expert recommendations and create a custom design plan.

2. Site Preparation: We carefully prepare your site, ensuring proper foundation and structural support for your new enclosure.

3. Frame Installation: Our skilled craftsmen install the frame using premium materials, ensuring structural integrity and longevity.

4. Professional Finishing: We complete your enclosure with attention to every detail, ensuring a beautiful, professional finish that enhances your property.

Why Choose SCREEN ARMORS for Construction?

Expert Craftsmanship: Our team has years of experience building screen enclosures that stand the test of time.

Premium Materials: We use only the highest quality materials to ensure durability and longevity.

Code Compliance: All our construction work meets or exceeds local building codes and regulations.

Custom Design: Every enclosure is designed specifically for your property and needs.
